Safety tips for overseas students in 2018 Winter vacation



To all the overseas students :

According to the school notice, the winter vacation in 2019 is from 21th Jan, to 24th Feb 2019, the time for the registration of next semester time is 23rd and 24th Feb 2019, courses begin on 25th Feb, 2019.

In order to ensure all the intl student’s safety in vacation and make sure all the students have a happy winter vacation. We call on all of you enhance your consciousness and capability of security and self-defense, avoid cheating, theft, car accidents, fires, food safety and other accidents. Specific reminders are as follows :

  • Strictly comply with the leaving and return registration system. Registrate at the College Counselors before leaving. Properly maintain dormitory items and take away or lock your valuables. Checking all electrical appliances before leaving, and paying attention to fire, closing doors and windows, ensuring the security　of dormitory. Stay in touch with your counselor while you are at home or not in campus, and register at your counselor immediately when you back to campus. Students who stay in campus should register, make sure that you can be connected and obey the management of the related departments. E-mail your School

  • Pay attention to traffic safety. During the winter vacation, the traffic is crowded, and there are lots of people, observe the traffic rules when you go home, go out, travel and return to school, and take care your items in travel. Carefully choose transport and travel agencies, do not take tractors, three-wheeled vehicles, low-speed trucks, assembled cars, scrapped cars and overloaded vehicles and other unsafe vehicles, and do not take the undocumented, unlicensed and illegal overload of travel.

  • Pay attention to personal safety. During the winter vacation, there are lots of parties and social activities, and you need to strengthen your safety awareness,to avoid accidental injuries caused by the crowded stampede, fireworks and firecrackers. You’d better go out with others in order to ensure that you can help each other when counter unexpected problems. Do not stay in some dangerous places, and do not take part in the dangerous activities. Do not go to places where there are few inhabitants round, and protect yourself when skating in rivers, lakes, beaches, reservoirs, etc. Avoid going outside late at night or when you are alone, especially girls. Do not drink too much or fight when gathering.

  • Do not organize or participate in illegal gatherings, demonstrations, demonstrations and other activities, not on the Internet to publish illegal speech, do not participate in various illegal activities.

  • Pay attention to food safety and disease prevention. Pay attention to food hygiene, try not to point at the roadside stalls dining, do not contact all kinds of infectious disease, buy and pay attention to the shelf-life of edible food, disease prevention work earnestly.

  • Improving the awareness of natural disaster prevention, and avoid the damage caused by various disasters.

  • Improve the ability to identify, guard against all kinds of temptations, and do not easily believe the information from strangers, network and mobile phone, do not believe rumors, transfer rumors, prevent fraud, personal property damage. Pay attention to hidden dangers around and inform the relevant departments when you have seen anything suspicious.

  • For those who do part-time job during the holiday, keep your personal prevention and ensure that the recruitment information from reliable sources, do not believe advertising in the streets, and the campus network. It is better to find a job through an intermediary company with a business license, and sign a formal labor contract with the employer. Not pay any deposit and do not mortgage any documents. Do not work in places of entertainment, avoid online cheating. Visa will be needed if you want a part-time job.

  • Students who stay in campus must obey the unified arrangement of the dormitory management center, strictly abide by the provisions of the management of student apartments, do the security work of fire prevention, especially electricity, no illegal use of electricity.
