International Student Union

(ISU)


The International Student Union (ISU) at UESTC works to help international students on their academic studies and campus life, help members cope with any difficulties they may encounter at UESTC, promote diversity on campus, promote good relationships between UESTC and its international program partners and create a memorable learning experience for all students with quality activites. ISU is open to all students with an interest in International culture, International activities and support of International students.
